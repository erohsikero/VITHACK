ABSTRACT

INTRODUCTION TO PROBLEM:

In 2015, an estimated 3,60,000 people died from drowning, making drowning a major public health problem worldwide. In 2018, injuries accounted for over 9% of total global mortality.Drowning is the 3rd leading cause of unintentional injury death worldwide, accounting for 7% of all injury-related deaths.There are an estimated 360 000 annual drowning deaths worldwide. India holds 6th place world wide and 2nd in Asia.The data on ocean deaths hit at some trends. People who die in local waters tend to be men and boys, typically under 30.

SOLUTION:

The footage from the HD camera with long focal length fixed in huge poles (similar to an night lamp post) in ocean were connected to YOLO neural network. These YOLO networks checks the footage frame by frame for detecting the people who were drowning in the ocean with the help of pre-trained weights(we personally collected from lifeguards and created own in our college swimming pool).when an person is detected his/her location is detected and plotted on map converted to an electric pulse as fast as thunder the signal flashes to the autonomous lifebuoy thrower gun ,Lifeguards/Rescue team, ambulance, police at same time . the autonomous lifebuoy thrower gun which has lifebuoy in form of bullet is fixed on the same pole as that of the camera fixed . the gun is tilted in correct position according to the drowning people and lifebuoy(airbag) is fired at them . the lifebuoy is fixed with an automatic air filling mechanism which fills the air in bag suddenly when it senses water . again the footage in the camera is checked to verify wheaten the drowning person caught the air bag or not , ones the drowning person got the airbag the jute attached with the pole and air bag is rotated in anticlockwise direction to pull the person back to the shore which helps to hold his life until the rescue team reaches him . then the man is treated with first aid kit which is placed in the pole itself (opens automatically when an emergency signal comes from the camera) and send to near by hospital with the help of ambulance . the information about the man is informed to his family by the police. the pole also has an flag hosting mechanism which hosted different color flag in beach ie, an double red flag is raised when the output from the wind sensor is very high indicating beach is closed for public use , yellow flag is hosted when an dangerous marine creature (shark , jellyfish , whale...) is spotted in the camera , an red yellow flag to indicate surfing is allowed and protected by life guard . the camera is also used for marine habitation , ie when an turtle is spotted in the camera its location is plotted on map and sent to rescue team so that the turtle nest on the sea sure can be spotted and secured . Also an LDR is kept at the top of the pole to detect the light intensity and to change the direction of solar panel which is used to charge the power bank used to operate the Rasberry pi module , also to switch on the light automatically during the night time.All these data and time stamp were stored in database for future access and improving accuracy. the data can be seen from the server room where air compressor is equipped to supply compressed air for the autonomous lifebuoy throwing gun to operate.
